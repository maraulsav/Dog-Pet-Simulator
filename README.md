## Description
In this website you can name your own dog. Feed them, pet them and make them fall asleep.
There are bar that show their current stats. If that bar is not fulfilled some action cannot be done.
Oh, but I am also really sorry that I can't yet save the database of your dog..

## How to play
1. Enter Your Dog Name 
2. Wait the loading Screen and you will end up in the main room
3. In the main room you can put your dog to sleep by clicking the bed emoji in the bottom of the screen
4. Not only that, in the main room you can pet your dog by clicking two times on the dog
5. You can go to the dining room by clicking the plate, fork and spoon emoji. Then to feed your dog click two times on the meat. 
6. Back to the living room by clicking the button back in top left corner
